Vanilla 3D Magic Cube

Experience fully 3D Rubik's cube rendered via HTML5 Canvas 2D API

Drag on canvas to rotate view

LAYER MOVES

Instructions

  • Rotate View: Click & Drag with your mouse on the canvas.
  • Rotate Layers: Use buttons or use Shift + Mouse drag directly on the cubelet.
  • Keyboard Controls: Press keys directly: U, D, L, R, F, B. Hold Shift for counter-clockwise.
